Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

Thank you for using the "Mad Yet" application. We take your privacy very seriously. This page is designed to inform you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

100% Local: "Mad Yet" is a completely local application. We do not collect, store, or upload any of your personal data or "vent" content to our servers. All vent records are stored encrypted in your device's local folder.

2. Data Storage and Backup

All your records (vent history) are saved on your device. Please note:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored encrypted in your device's local folder, protected by iOS file protection mechanisms.
  • System Backup: Your data is automatically included in iOS system backups (iTunes or iCloud backups). When you restore your device or switch to a new device, your vent records can be restored with the backup. All backup data is encrypted and protected by Apple.
  • Data Loss: If you delete the app or clear local cache without a backup, this data will be permanently lost. We cannot recover this data for you.
  • Our Commitment: We do not collect, upload, or access any of your data. Your privacy is entirely controlled by you and Apple's backup system.

3. External Links

This app may contain links to third-party services (such as Apple Maps). When you click these links, you will leave this app and be subject to that third-party service's privacy policy.

4. Changes

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We recommend that you review this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us: support@fenglema-app.newtree.tech

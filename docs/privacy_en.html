Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2026

Thank you for using the "Mad Yet" application. We take your privacy very seriously. This page is designed to inform you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

100% Local: "Mad Yet" keeps your emotional records local. We do not collect, store, or upload your "vent" content to our servers. All vent records are stored encrypted in your device's local folder.

2. Data Storage and Backup

All your records (vent history) are saved on your device. Please note:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored encrypted in your device's local folder, protected by iOS file protection mechanisms.
  • System Backup: Your data is automatically included in iOS system backups (iTunes or iCloud backups). When you restore your device or switch to a new device, your vent records can be restored with the backup. All backup data is encrypted and protected by Apple.
  • Data Loss: If you delete the app or clear local cache without a backup, this data will be permanently lost. We cannot recover this data for you.
  • Our Commitment: We do not collect, upload, or access your vent content. Your vent history is controlled by you and Apple's backup system.
  • History Lock: You may enable "require unlock before opening history." Authentication is handled locally by iOS, and we never access biometric data.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Partners

The app integrates Google AdMob. Ads are currently shown on the optional support page, but the Google Mobile Ads SDK may initialize when the app opens.

Google and its advertising partners may collect or receive advertising-related information from your device, including device identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, ad interactions, diagnostics, and performance data. This information may be used to deliver ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with applicable law. Google handles this information according to its own policies and your device or account privacy settings.

We do not send your vent text, tags, chaos levels, calm session history, or local vent history to Google or other advertising services.

You can control ad tracking and personalization through iOS privacy settings and any Google consent or ad personalization controls available in your region.

4. External Links

This app may contain links to third-party services (such as Apple Maps). When you click these links, you will leave this app and be subject to that third-party service's privacy policy.

5. Changes

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We recommend that you review this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us: support@fenglema-app.newtree.tech

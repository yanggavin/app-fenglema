Why Choose Mad Yet

🎯

Minimalist Design

One question, two choices. Simple and direct emotional check-in without complex processes, focusing on your current feelings.

🔒

Privacy First

Vent content is encrypted and saved on your device. The app supports advertising, but it does not upload your local vent history.

📝

Editable Vent Journal

Save, edit, and delete vents anytime, then organize them with tags for fast filtering and review.

🫁

1-Minute Calm

When emotions spike, start a 60-second breathing and grounding flow before taking the next step.

🔐

History Lock

Optionally require device authentication before opening vent history for stronger privacy on shared devices.

💙

Gentle Companion

When you need help, the app recommends nearby counseling centers or relaxation spots.

Your Privacy, Our Commitment

We understand the private nature of emotional records. Mad Yet stores vent content encrypted on your device and protects it with iOS system backups. The app supports advertising, but it does not send your vent text or local vent history to ad services.
